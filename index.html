Zionsville moves forward with new fire station

The Town of Zionsville is moving ahead with plans for a new fire station to support growing areas on the west side of town. The facility will expand emergency response coverage and replace the Perry Township Volunteer Fire Department station currently serving the area.

Plans call for a modern station with multiple apparatus bays, living quarters for firefighters, and space for future growth. Town leaders say the project will strengthen public safety infrastructure as the community continues to grow.
