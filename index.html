Zionsville’s Largest Park Set to Open in 2026

The Carpenter Nature Preserve, a 215-acre park being developed on the former Wolf Run Golf Course site, is scheduled to open its first phase in May 2026. One of the preserve’s most distinctive features is its approximately 4,000 linear feet of Eagle Creek, which will shape much of the landscape and provide scenic views along planned walking trails. Initial amenities will include trail access, parking, and restrooms, making the space easy for residents to enjoy from day one. When fully developed, the preserve will become the largest park in Boone County, offering miles of paved and natural-surface trails, overlooks, and year-round opportunities to connect with nature. Future phases of the project include a nature center, expanded trail networks, boardwalks, and enhanced wetlands and woodlands designed for hiking, wildlife viewing, and outdoor learning.

Location: Intersection of US-421 and US-32

Sources: Zionsville Parks & Recreation – Carpenter Nature Preserve https://www.zionsville-in.gov/733/Carpenter-Nature-Preserve
