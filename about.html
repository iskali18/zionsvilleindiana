About

A simple guide to Zionsville

ZionsvilleIndiana.com is a photo-forward, curated guide to local events and highlights — built for people who want to know what’s going on without digging through ten different calendars.

What you’ll find here

A few focused categories that are easy to keep current.

Upcoming events

A curated calendar plus a few highlighted picks — family-friendly, community-focused, and seasonal.

Tip: If you only update one thing weekly, update the highlights.

Local guides

Downtown, parks & trails, and simple “what to do” ideas you can use anytime.

These pages are evergreen — add to them gradually.

Photo highlights

The visual side of town — seasonal scenes, community moments, and places worth wandering.

All photos are original unless otherwise credited.

How curation works

Short, human, and local-first.

What gets included

  • Town and community events
  • Seasonal traditions and family-friendly activities
  • Live music, markets, and special happenings
  • Local highlights worth sharing

What this is not

  • Every single event in the region
  • Pay-to-play listings disguised as recommendations
  • A replacement for official town or venue announcements
