About

Welcome to ZionsvilleIndiana.com

Zionsville is a small-town-feeling community just northwest of Indianapolis, known for its walkable downtown, seasonal events, and family-friendly vibe. This site is a simple guide to help residents and visitors find the “big stuff” worth planning around—without scrolling through clutter.

What you’ll find here

  • Featured community events
    Medium-to-large events (festivals, parades, concerts, seasonal celebrations).
  • Seasonal highlights
    A few curated ideas each season—updated quarterly.
  • Local guides
    Parks & trails, downtown favorites, and easy family outings.
  • Headline roundup
    A short list of links to official or reputable sources.

How events are chosen

To keep things clean and useful, the calendar focuses on events that are likely to draw hundreds or thousands of people.

  • Included
    Town-wide celebrations, festivals, parades, major markets, concerts, big fundraisers.
  • Usually excluded
    Small meetups, recurring classes, small club events, niche gatherings.
  • Nearby towns
    Occasionally included if it’s a big, broadly appealing event within a short drive.
