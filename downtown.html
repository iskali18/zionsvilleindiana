Downtown Zionsville

Brick streets, local shops, cozy cafés, and an unhurried pace — downtown Zionsville is a place to slow down. Whether you have an hour or an evening, it’s easy to wander, explore, and enjoy the small moments that make the village feel special.

Ways to spend an afternoon or evening

Four easy “choose-your-own” plans. Mix and match — or take your own route.

Daytime
A daytime outing with family
Rosie’s Place → Lions Park (playgrounds + trail) → Creekside Nature Park → The Scoop.
Best for: morning to early afternoon
Evening
An easy, elevated evening
Dinner at Noah Grant’s, Convivio, or Cobblestone → Main Street stroll → The Scoop.
Best for: dusk and later
Afternoon
A relaxed afternoon wander
Our Place Coffee or Roasted in the Village → shop browse → Truffles & Creams (before close).
Best for: daytime (many close early)
Anytime
Discovery mode
Side streets + details → pop into new-to-you shops → take-home finds at The Baker’s House and Angelo’s Italian Market.
Best for: slower weekdays + photo walks
