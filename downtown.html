Downtown Zionsville

Brick streets, local shops, cozy cafés, and seasonal events — this is a curated starting point. I’ll keep it simple: what to do, where to wander, and ideas for a great evening or weekend.

Quick start

If you only have an hour or two, start here.

Easy stroll

  • Walk Main Street and browse shops at an unhurried pace.
  • Grab a warm drink and people-watch for a bit.
  • Snap photos — the brick + storefronts are made for it.

Great evening

  • Dinner downtown, then a dessert stop.
  • Check the calendar for a live music night or seasonal event.
  • End with a sunset walk through the village.
