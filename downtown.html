Downtown & Beyond

Brick streets, local shops, cozy cafés, and an unhurried pace. Downtown Zionsville is a place to slow down — with a few favorite spots just beyond the village worth a short drive.

Three easy ways to spend time around town

Flexible ideas you can enjoy at your own pace.

Start with a kid-friendly meal at Greek’s Pizzeria. Head to nearby Lions Park for playgrounds and an easy walking loop, then continue into Creekside Nature Park. End with ice cream at The Scoop.
Greek’s → Lions Park → Creekside → The Scoop
Family time & fresh air
Begin with coffee at Our Place or Roasted in the Village, both best enjoyed earlier in the day. Wander Main Street shops, then stop at Rosie’s Place for brunch or an early lunch. Pick up something sweet at Truffles & Creams or Angelo’s Italian Market.
Coffee → Rosie’s → Truffles or Angelo’s
A slow morning downtown
Enjoy a sit-down dinner at Noah Grant’s, Convivio, or Cobblestone. Afterward, stroll Main Street and enjoy the brick streets after dark. Finish with a relaxed stop at The Scoop.
Noah Grant’s / Convivio / Cobblestone
