This printable downtown Zionsville map shows [restaurants](/articles/downtown-zionsville-restaurants), cafés, shops, public parking, restrooms, and other landmarks throughout the Village. Download the PDF before your visit or save the JPEG to your phone for a quick reference while walking downtown.

The map covers the brick-paved section of Main Street from Poplar Street to Sycamore Street, along with the nearby side streets where most restaurants, shops, galleries, and public parking lots are located. Main Street forms the center of the Village, with restaurants concentrated along the brick-paved blocks and shops extending onto nearby side streets.

Businesses are numbered and color-coded by category, making it easy to find places to eat, browse local shops, or locate parking during your visit.

## Parking

The map marks two public parking lots along Main Street, including the Main and Sycamore lot with public restrooms. Street parking is also found throughout the Village, and nearby Lions Park has free parking except during Fall Festival and some special events.

For the full parking map, see the [parking section on the Downtown Zionsville page](/downtown#parking).
