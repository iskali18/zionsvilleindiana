## Parking

The map marks two public parking lots along Main Street, including the Main and Sycamore lot with public restrooms. Street parking is also found throughout the Village, and nearby Lions Park has free parking except during Fall Festival and some special events.

For the full parking map, see the [parking section on the Downtown Zionsville page](/downtown#parking).
