Shopping in downtown Zionsville is concentrated in the Village, where brick-paved streets, small storefronts, and locally owned shops give the district its walkable feel. Most shops are on or near Main Street, Cedar Street, Pine Street, Poplar Street, and First Street, with antiques and a few specialty stops a little farther from the main shopping blocks.

This guide groups downtown Zionsville shops by category, including boutiques, home goods, gifts, flowers, books, jewelry, art, specialty foods, hobby shops, and vintage finds.

## Clothing & Boutiques {#clothing-boutiques}

Downtown clothing shops cover everyday boutique pieces, activewear, accessories, prom and event dresses, bridal gowns, and couture evening wear. Most are clustered on Main Street, Pine Street, and First Street, so the section can be covered on foot.

- Boutique 33 carries women's clothing, jewelry, and accessories in a small East Pine Street storefront.

### Brick Street Bridal
- **Address:** 40 N. Main St
- **Website:** [brickstreetbridal.com](https://brickstreetbridal.com/)
- Brick Street Bridal is a private bridal boutique offering wedding dresses and accessories in an appointment-based setting.

### Duo Boutique
- **Address:** 150 S. Main St
- **Local guide:** [Duo Boutique](/businesses/duo-boutique)

## Food & Drink {#food-drink}

Food-and-drink shopping downtown is built around take-home treats and ingredients: fresh pasta, wine, butcher cuts, pies, cookies, chocolate, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. These shops cover gifts, dinner supplies, and sweets to bring home.

### Angelo's Italian Market
- **Address:** 95 S. First St
- **Local guide:** [Angelo's Italian Market](/businesses/angelos-italian-market)
- Angelo’s Italian Market sells fresh pasta, sauces, meatballs, prepared meals, breads, Italian imports, and grab-and-go items for take-home meals.

### Grapevine Cottage
- **Address:** 61 S. Main St
- **Website:** [grapevinecottage.com](https://www.grapevinecottage.com/)
- Grapevine Cottage is a wine shop with wine, gift baskets, artisan bread, cheese, beer, crackers, snacks, and wine accessories.

### Moody's Butcher Shop
- **Address:** 20 E. Cedar St
- **Website:** [moodysbutchershop.com](https://moodysbutchershop.com/zionsville/)
- Moody’s Butcher Shop is a full-service butcher with cut-to-order beef, pork, poultry, seafood, charcuterie, wine, craft beer, and market items.

### My Sugar Pie
- **Address:** 40 E. Pine St
- **Local guide:** [My Sugar Pie](/businesses/my-sugar-pie)
- My Sugar Pie bakes gourmet homemade pies in fruit, cream, classic, and seasonal flavors, sold whole and by the slice.

### Not As Famous Cookie Co.
- **Address:** 30 E. Poplar St
- **Website:** [notasfamouscookie.square.site](https://notasfamouscookie.square.site/)
- Not As Famous Cookie Co. sells gourmet cookies, mini bites, cookie trays, and cookie cakes for pickup, delivery, and shipping.

### Truffles & Creams
- **Address:** 55 E. Pine St
- **Local guide:** [Truffles & Creams](/businesses/truffles-and-creams)

### Zionsville Olive Oil

- **Website:** [zionsvilleoliveoil.com](https://www.zionsvilleoliveoil.com/)

## Crafts, Hobbies & Specialty {#crafts-specialty}

Downtown also has shops for project supplies, classes, games, hobbies, bikes, and hands-on gifts. This section covers places where visitors can make a terrarium, learn mahjong, buy yarn, repair a bike, or browse stamps and coins.

### Abby's Garden Parties
- **Address:** 40 E. Poplar St
- **Website:** [abbysgardenparties.com](https://www.abbysgardenparties.com/)
- Abby’s Garden Parties is a DIY terrarium bar and plant shop where visitors build custom terrariums using their own pot, plants, and decorations.

### eBoom Electric Bikes
- **Address:** 47 S. Main St
- **Website:** [eboomelectricbikes.com](https://www.eboomelectricbikes.com/)
- eBoom Electric Bikes sells e-bikes, e-trikes, cycling gear, and accessories, with bike service and repairs for electric and traditional bikes.

### Meet at Mahj Studio & Boutique
- **Address:** 260 S. First St, Suite 4
- **Website:** [meetatmahj.com](https://www.meetatmahj.com/)
- Meet at Mahj is a mahjong studio and boutique offering lessons, open play, leagues, private parties, tiles, mats, racks, bags, gifts, and accessories.

### Persnickety Stitchers
- **Address:** 58A N. Main St
- **Website:** [persnicketystitchers.com](https://www.persnicketystitchers.com/)
- Persnickety Stitchers carries cross-stitch and needlepoint supplies, specialty threads, canvases, patterns, and stitching accessories.

### The Village Yarn Company
- **Address:** 209 S. Main St
- **Local guide:** [The Village Yarn Company](/businesses/village-yarn-company)
- The Village Yarn Company carries yarns from around the world, plus knitting and crochet supplies, patterns, classes, and private lessons.

### Village Stamp and Coin
- **Address:** 115 N. Main St

### Watch Us Weave Studio
- **Address:** 45 S. Elm St
- **Website:** [watchusfarm.com](https://www.watchusfarm.com/)
- Watch Us Weave Studio is a fiber arts studio from Watch Us Farm, supporting adults with intellectual disabilities through weaving, handmade goods, classes, and community programs.

## Books {#books}

Downtown’s bookshops are small, independent places for browsing: one with rare, collectible, used, new, and local-author books, and one with a mission-driven new-book selection.

### Black Dog Books
- **Address:** 115 S. Main St
- **Local guide:** [Black Dog Books](/businesses/black-dog-books)
- Black Dog Books carries rare and collectible books, fine bindings, gently read and new books, local-author titles, and general stock across many subjects.

### Curious Squirrel Books
- **Address:** 120 S. Main St
- **Website:** [curioussquirrelbooks.com](https://www.curioussquirrelbooks.com/)
- Curious Squirrel Books is a mission-driven independent bookshop with a diverse selection and an emphasis on marginalized voices and underrepresented stories.

## Art & Galleries {#art-galleries}

Downtown galleries and studios make art part of the shopping district, from co-op gallery pieces and custom framing to working artist studios on Main and Cedar.

### A King's Art Studio and Gallery
- **Address:** 60 E. Cedar St
- A King’s Art Studio and Gallery is a Cedar Street art studio and gallery featuring original artwork and photography.

### Art IN Hand Gallery
- **Address:** 211 S. Main St
- **Website:** [artinhandgallery.com](https://www.artinhandgallery.com/)
- Art IN Hand Gallery is an artist-owned cooperative gallery with work by local artists, including jewelry, painting, woodworking, printmaking, fused glass, photography, pottery, wood carving, and painted silk.

### CV Art and Frame / Thomas Kinkade Gallery
- **Address:** 110 S. Main St
- **Website:** [cvartandframe.com](https://cvartandframe.com/)
- CV Art and Frame offers fine art sales, custom framing, and restoration, with the adjoining Thomas Kinkade Gallery on Main Street.

### Laforge Art
- **Address:** 70 S. Main St
- Laforge Art showcases the vibrant mixed-media and acrylic work of Zionsville artist Laura LaForge.

### Out of the Blue Art Studio
- **Address:** 70 S. Main St
- **Website:** [outofthebluezionsville.com](https://outofthebluezionsville.com/)
- Out of the Blue Art Studio is a working studio for porcelain, oil on canvas, and mixed-media art, with classes and workshops for youth and adults.

## A Note for Outdoor Lovers

[Moving Waters Outfitters](https://www.mwoutfitters.com/) is a fly fishing and kayak outfitter located at 675 S.
