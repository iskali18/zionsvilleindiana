Zionsville has coffee shops for different kinds of visits — quiet downtown cafés, gluten-free baked goods, cold brew with a drive-thru, small-batch roasting, and sit-down spaces outside the Village. This guide covers coffee shops in Zionsville and nearby Whitestown, with notes on parking, atmosphere, and what each place is known for.

Hours change with seasons and holidays. Check each shop's [Google listing](https://www.google.com/maps) or official site for current times before heading out.

## Downtown Zionsville Coffee Shops

### Our Place Coffee

**Address:** 65 S 1st St, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** Street and nearby public lots

[Our Place Coffee](/businesses/our-place-coffee) is an independent café just off Main Street, intentionally built around the idea of being a welcoming gathering spot — a place to come in, sit down, and reconnect with friends, family, or a book rather than scroll on a phone. The owners describe the food menu as eclectic, with a wide range of breakfast and lunch options including a build-your-own sandwich. The space is roomy with comfy seating, and groups regularly settle in for an hour or two.

### Roasted in the Village

**Address:** 27 E Pine St, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** Street and nearby public lots

Roasted in the Village is a downtown coffee shop on East Pine Street, close to Main Street shops and restaurants. The space features a wall of artwork by local artists, much of it available for purchase. The shop is known for its rotating roster of house-made specialty syrups — flavors like maple brown sugar and orange cardamom alongside seasonal additions — used in lattes and other drinks. Coffee comes from PT's Coffee Roasting Co., and the tea selection is sourced from Indiana-based Nelson's Tea.

## Local Coffee Shops Outside the Village

### RIVET Coffee Bar & Roastery

**Address:** 10850 Creek Way, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** On-site lot

RIVET Coffee Bar & Roastery is on Creek Way inside the Graham Rahal Performance campus, situated between the Ducati dealership and Graham Rahal Performance. The setting is what sets it apart — a coffee bar inside a large, sleek facility where exotic and high-performance cars are displayed nearby. RIVET roasts its beans in-house and offers a substantial food menu including pearl sugar Liège waffles, frittatas, and open-faced sandwiches alongside the coffee and espresso menu. The room has a bustling, dining-area energy with a large number of tables.

### Aspasia Coffee and Bake Shop

**Address:** 3091 US-421 N, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** On-site lot

Aspasia Coffee and Bake Shop is a dedicated gluten-free coffee and bake shop on US-421. The baked goods are prepared in a separate kitchen rather than only labeled gluten-free, making it a dependable stop for coffee with gluten-free pastries, desserts, and take-home items.

### Mochaland

**Address:** 10895 N Michigan Rd, Suite 120, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** On-site lot

Mochaland is a newer coffee and bakery spot on Michigan Road near Bennett Parkway, focused on small-batch roasted coffee. The shop adds another local coffee option to the Michigan Road corridor outside the Village.

## Drive-Thru and Chain Coffee

For drivers who want a quick coffee stop or a familiar menu, Zionsville has several chain coffee options.

### Java House

**Address:** 11150 N Michigan Rd, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** On-site lot with drive-thru

Java House is an Indiana-based coffee chain on Michigan Road. The menu centers on cold brew (coffee steeped cold for many hours, then served from a tap for a smooth, low-bitterness cup), served hot, iced, or as specialty drinks. Cold foams (a light, frothy topping that adds flavor and a creamy layer without fully mixing into the drink) come in rotating flavors. With a drive-thru, it works for a quick stop outside the Village.

Java House also offers **decaf cold brew** for anyone who wants the cold brew experience without the caffeine.

### Starbucks

Three Starbucks locations sit in and around Zionsville, all with a drive-thru:

- **Oak Street, Zionsville** — drive-thru; a common after-school study spot during the school year
- **Holliday Farms** — drive-thru
- **Whitestown** — drive-thru, close to the west side of town

## Coffee Roasters and Take-Home Coffee

### Julian Coffee Roasters

**Address:** 10830 Bennett Pkwy, Suite N, Zionsville, IN
**Parking:** On-site lot

Julian Coffee Roasters is a retail roaster on Bennett Parkway rather than a sit-down café. Stop in for whole-bean or ground coffee, cold-brew growlers, and brewing gear. The shop roasts in small batches and supplies coffee to other shops in the area. Weekday-only hours, so plan a take-home run accordingly.

## Nearby Coffee Shops

### Hattie's Coffee House (Whitestown)

**Address:** 607 S Main St, Whitestown, IN
**Parking:** On-site lot

Hattie's Coffee House sits in a restored farmhouse in the center of Whitestown, with several seating nooks, outdoor seating, and a family-run feel. Drinks are the main offering, with toasted bagel sandwiches available as a light option, and the coffee is roasted by The Daily Grind. The space is used for community events as well as everyday coffee runs. Hattie's is named after Harriet White, wife of the town's namesake.

## Worth the Drive

If you are willing to go a little farther, two neighboring towns have coffee worth the trip.

### Carmel — about 20–25 minutes east

- **Indie Coffee Roasters** (220 E Main St) — in-house roaster in the Arts & Design District, in a renovated-house setting.
- **Prufrock Coffee Company** (275 Veterans Way) — a two-level Midtown café near the Monon Trail. The upstairs is bright and social; the downstairs is a quiet, book-lined study lounge. The Spanish latte (espresso with sweetened condensed milk, for a richer, less acidic cup) stands out on the menu.
- **Bovaconti Coffee** — Italian-inspired café in the Arts & Design District with outdoor seating.
- **MOTW Coffee & Pastries** — a large, bright space with a wide menu of specialty lattes.

### Brownsburg — about 20 minutes west

- **7 Brew Coffee** — a fast drive-thru-only concept with a wide drink menu.
- **Best Friends Coffee & Bagels** — a Main Street spot with several seating rooms, free Wi-Fi, and bagel sandwiches.
- **Timber City Coffee** (Hepburn Circle) — an independent café with coffee and homemade cupcakes.
