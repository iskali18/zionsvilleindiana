The Zionsville Street Dance is an annual outdoor concert and community gathering hosted by the Zionsville Chamber of Commerce on Main Street in downtown Zionsville. The ticketed event brings thousands of people to the Village for live music, local drinks, food trucks, and family-friendly activities on the historic brick street.

The 2026 Street Dance takes place Saturday, August 1, from 6:00 to 10:00 PM, with gates opening at 5:00 PM.

## What to expect

The Street Dance turns Main Street into an outdoor summer concert venue. The stage is set up at the north end of Main Street, with music, food, drinks, and event activity spread through the downtown area.

The 2026 lineup features The Dundies as the headliner, with Pigeons of Market Square returning as the opening act.

Local breweries, wineries, and distilleries serve drinks throughout the event, and food trucks offer a range of dinner and snack options. Families can also find a kids' area on-site.

Main Street is closed to vehicles during the event. Pre-purchased tickets are scanned at the gate beginning at 5:00 PM, and on-site ticket sales are also available.

## Plan your visit

**When:** Saturday, August 1, 2026, 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM; gates open at 5:00 PM  
**Location:** Main Street, downtown Zionsville  
**Admission:** Ticketed. Advance general admission is $25, gate admission is $30 for ages 11 and up, and children 10 and under are $10. VIP tickets are $75 and must be purchased in advance. Advance tickets go on sale June 1.  
**Family:** Kids' area on-site  

**Parking:** Main Street is closed for the event, so plan to park nearby and walk into the event area. The largest available public lot is at the northwest corner of Main Street and Sycamore Road, with additional parking at Lions Park and on side streets around the Village. Spaces near the event fill up before the 5:00 PM gate opening. See the [downtown parking map](/downtown#parking) for details.

**Food and drinks:** Local breweries, wineries, distilleries, and food trucks are on-site. Most vendors accept cards. ID is checked for alcohol purchases.

## Hosts

The Zionsville Street Dance is hosted by the [Zionsville Chamber of Commerce](https://business.zionsvillechamber.org/calendar/Details/zionsville-street-dance-2026-1687242). The event is the Chamber's largest annual fundraiser and one of the biggest summer gatherings in downtown Zionsville.
