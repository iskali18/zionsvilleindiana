Brick Street Market is a premier outdoor artisan market that has been a staple of the Zionsville spring calendar for decades. Held annually on the first Saturday in May, the event transforms the historic brick-paved Main Street into an open-air gallery for over 140 curated vendors. It is organized by the Zionsville Chamber of Commerce and serves as one of the town’s largest community gatherings, attracting visitors from across Central Indiana for a day of outdoor shopping and local dining.

The market provides an opportunity to support independent makers while exploring the permanent boutiques and restaurants that line the Village. Because the event occupies several blocks of the downtown area, the town implements specific traffic plans and street closures. The event is held rain or shine, reflecting the community’s commitment to this long-standing local tradition.
