Sip, Stroll & Shop is an evening shopping event in downtown Zionsville, organized by Main Street Zionsville. Shops throughout the Village welcome visitors with treats, specials, surprises, and seasonal offerings. There is no set route — visitors move through the Village at their own pace and choose which shops to visit.

Because the event runs into the evening, it can be an evening out with friends, a casual date night, or a reason to spend a few hours downtown after work. Many visitors add dinner, drinks, or dessert downtown before or after shopping.

## Dates and details

- Thursday, April 9, 2026
- Thursday, June 11, 2026
- Thursday, September 10, 2026

All three 2026 dates run from 4:00 to 8:00 PM.

**Location:** Downtown Zionsville, along Main Street and nearby blocks  
**Admission:** Free

Participating shops and their specials may vary by date, so check Main Street Zionsville before heading downtown. Parking is available along Main Street and nearby side streets, with public lots at the northwest corner of Main Street and Sycamore Road and the southwest corner of Main Street and Pine Street.
