The Zionsville Fourth of July Celebration at Lions Park is one of the town’s most well-attended annual events. Coordinated by the Zionsville Lions Club, the celebration draws families to Lions Park for an afternoon and evening with live music, food vendors, bounce houses, and activities for all ages. The park fills with people gathering on the grass ahead of the fireworks display. After dark, a professionally choreographed fireworks show is launched from within the park, visible throughout the surrounding area and concluding the evening.
