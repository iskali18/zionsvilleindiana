The Sunday Summer Concerts are free evening concerts held at the Lions Park bandstand in Zionsville and hosted by the Zionsville Lions Club. The series runs on five Sundays from July 5 through August 2, 2026, with performances beginning at 7:00 PM.

This Sunday series is separate from the Zionsville Cultural District’s Wednesday evening Summer Concert Series.

## 2026 Lineup

| Date | Band | Conductor/Director |
|------|------|----------|
| Sunday, July 5 | Zionsville Concert Band | John Richardson |
| Sunday, July 12 | Indianapolis Symphonic Band | John Hilmer |
| Sunday, July 19 | New Horizons Band | John Marshall |
| Sunday, July 26 | Indianapolis Municipal Band | John Marshall |
| Sunday, August 2 | GM Horns Big Band | Edward Zlaty |

## Plan your visit

**When:** Sundays, July 5 – August 2, 2026  
**Concerts:** 7:00 PM  
**Location:** Lions Park, 11053 Sycamore St, Zionsville  
**Admission:** Free; food and beverage are available for a donation

**Parking:** Free parking is available at Lions Park. Additional public parking may be available in nearby Village lots and along surrounding streets.

## What to bring

Bring a lawn chair or blanket for seating on the grass. Food and beverage are available for a donation.

For more information, visit the [Zionsville Lions Club concert page](https://www.zionsvillelions.com/concerts-in-july-1).
