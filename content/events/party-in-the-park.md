Party in the Park is Zionsville's free end-of-school-year celebration, held on the last day of school at Mulberry Fields Park. Hosted by Zionsville Parks and Recreation along with the Zionsville Nature Center and the Town of Zionsville, the event marks the kickoff to summer for local families with three hours of activities, food, and play.

## What's planned for 2026

The afternoon includes a wide mix of activities for kids and families:

- **Splash pad** open at Mulberry Fields — a great way to cool off
- **Live DJ** spinning music throughout the afternoon
- **Food trucks** on-site
- **Touch-a-Truck** with the Zionsville Fire and Police Departments
- **Zionsville Nature Center** special appearance with hands-on activities
- **Games and activities** throughout the park

## Plan your visit

**When:** Thursday, May 28, 2026 (last day of school)  
**Hours:** 3:00 PM – 6:00 PM  
**Location:** [Mulberry Fields Park](/parks/mulberry-fields), Zionsville  
**Admission:** Free  
**Parking:** Mulberry Fields Park has its own parking lot.

Bring swimsuits, towels, and a change of clothes for kids who want to use the splash pad. Sunscreen and water bottles are good ideas for the warm late-May afternoon. Food trucks operate independently, so bring cash or cards if you plan to eat there.

## After the event

Mulberry Fields Park is a short drive from downtown Zionsville. [The Scoop](/businesses/the-scoop) on South Main Street is a natural follow-up for kids who still have energy at 6 PM, and [Greek's Pizzeria](/businesses/greeks-pizzeria) or [Zionsville Pizzeria](/businesses/zionsville-pizzeria) are easy family dinner options on Main Street.
