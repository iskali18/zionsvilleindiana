Mulberry Fields is one of Zionsville's more scenic open spaces, known particularly for its meadows and the quality of its evening light — the featured hero photo on this site's previous version was taken here at sunset.

## What's here

Open meadow areas, walking paths, and a setting that feels more expansive and unhurried than the town's more developed parks. The natural landscape and open sky make it a favorite for people who want space to breathe.

## Best for

Evening walks, photography, families who want open space without playground crowds, and anyone who appreciates a landscape that hasn't been heavily developed.
