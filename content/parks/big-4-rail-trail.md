The Big 4 Rail Trail is one of Zionsville's most-used outdoor spaces — a paved, multi-use path that runs through town and connects to the broader regional trail network. The long, straight stretches make it ideal for steady movement: running, cycling, or a brisk walk without the winding terrain of the town's natural parks.

## Who it's for

The paved surface and flat grade make this accessible to nearly everyone — joggers, cyclists, inline skaters, and families with strollers. It's the trail locals use for everyday exercise rather than exploration.

## Access & parking

Multiple access points throughout Zionsville. Street parking is available near key access points along the route.

## Connecting your trip

After a morning on the trail, Roasted in the Village and Our Place Coffee are both easy stops in the downtown area. Both close in the early afternoon, so plan accordingly.
