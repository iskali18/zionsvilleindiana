Lions Park is the most central and frequently used park in Zionsville, located just off Main Street near downtown. Its flat paved loop, playgrounds, and open green space make it a go-to for families, casual walkers, and community events.

## What's here

The park includes several playground structures suited to different age groups, a paved walking loop that works well for strollers and casual walkers, and open lawn areas for picnics or recreation. Restrooms are available.

## Community events

Lions Park is home to several annual community events including the EGGnormous Egg Hunt each spring and various seasonal gatherings. The park is also where Lincoln Park's historical significance to the town is nearby — Abraham Lincoln once spoke here, and his funeral train passed through Zionsville.

## Connecting your visit

Creekside Nature Park is directly adjacent — an easy extension if you want a quieter, more natural walk after visiting Lions Park. Downtown Zionsville is a short walk away for coffee or a meal after.
