Starkey Park is Zionsville's most substantial natural area for serious trail exploration. An extensive network of wooded paths runs along Eagle Creek, offering a genuine nature experience within the town's limits.

## What to expect

Unlike the flat, paved experience at Lions Park or the Big 4 Rail Trail, Starkey involves real terrain — some routes have meaningful elevation changes and the natural surface trails require more attention underfoot. A trail map is helpful; pay attention to markers at intersections.

## Who it's for

Best suited to hikers, trail runners, and nature enthusiasts comfortable with uneven terrain. The wooded setting and creek proximity make it excellent for wildlife observation. Not ideal for strollers or young children without attentive supervision on the more rugged sections.

## Seasonal notes

Trails can be muddy after rain. Spring and fall offer the best conditions and scenery.
