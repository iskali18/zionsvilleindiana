Creekside Nature Park sits directly adjacent to Lions Park and offers a quieter, more natural experience than its well-known neighbor. Short trails wind through wooded areas alongside the creek, making it a natural extension of any visit to Lions Park.

## What's here

The park is small and focused — a short loop through trees and along the creek. It's not a destination on its own for most people, but it pairs naturally with Lions Park and provides a calmer, more nature-oriented experience for kids who enjoy exploring.

## Best for

Families with younger children, people wanting a short natural walk after the Lions Park playground, or anyone who wants a brief quiet moment before heading into downtown.
