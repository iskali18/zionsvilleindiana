The Zionsville Nature Center is part of the town's Parks and Recreation department, providing educational programming and resources focused on the natural environment. It's one of the less visible parts of Zionsville's parks system but a meaningful resource for families and school groups.

## What's here

The Nature Center offers structured programming for children and families, nature exhibits, and resources tied to the town's broader parks and natural areas. Programming varies by season — check the Parks & Recreation department website for current schedules and registration.

## Best for

Families with children who want a more structured nature education experience, school groups, and residents interested in local ecology and environmental programming.

## Getting more information

For current programs, hours, and registration, visit the Zionsville Parks & Recreation department directly through zionsville-in.gov.
