The Friendly Tavern is a longtime gathering place on Main Street in Zionsville, serving American tavern fare in a neighborhood bar setting. Its tagline — "you are a stranger here but once" — sums up the regulars-and-newcomers feel of the room.

The tavern is **21 and over**; guests aged 18 and older are permitted when accompanied by a parent or guardian.

## The menu

The menu covers American tavern classics including hand-breaded tenderloin sandwiches, chicken wings, breaded chicken, steaks, prime rib, and a range of sandwiches and salads. The kitchen is known for its hand-breaded tenderloin sandwich and chicken wings.

## Planning your visit

The Friendly Tavern is open for lunch and dinner Monday through Saturday, with later evening hours on Fridays and Saturdays. It is **closed on Sundays**. The tavern is at 290 S. Main Street; check the current hours on its [Google Maps listing](https://www.google.com/maps/place/290+S+Main+St,+Zionsville,+IN+46077) before visiting.
