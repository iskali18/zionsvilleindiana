The Friendly Tavern, known to locals as **The Friendly**, is a longtime gathering place on Main Street in Zionsville, serving American tavern fare in a neighborhood bar setting. Its tagline — "You are a stranger here but once" — sums up the regulars-and-newcomers feel of the room.

The tavern is **21 and over**; guests aged 18 and older are permitted when accompanied by a parent or guardian. It serves lunch and dinner Monday through Saturday and is **closed on Sundays**.

## Menu

The Friendly Tavern's wings are the restaurant's signature item, with many diners also recommending the hand-breaded tenderloin sandwich. The menu features classic American tavern fare, from burgers and sandwiches to steaks, seafood, and desserts.
