Greek's Pizzeria has been a reliable fixture of Zionsville's dining scene for years, located on the northern end of the brick-paved Main Street. The restaurant is known for its signature garlic butter-brushed crust and a wide variety of specialty pizzas, ranging from traditional favorites to unique gourmet combinations like the "Cordon Bleu a la Rafael."

The menu also includes fresh-baked breadsticks, salads, and a selection of Italian sandwiches and pasta dishes. The Zionsville location offers a casual dining environment with both indoor seating and a seasonal outdoor patio that overlooks the village sidewalk. Carry-out and delivery services are also available.
