Black Dog Books is an independent bookshop housed in a historic 1850s building on Zionsville's Main Street. The shop maintains a specialized inventory of rare and antiquarian volumes, with a particular focus on history, poetry, fine bindings, and Indiana-specific titles. 

Beyond its retail collection, the shop provides professional services such as custom book searches and expert repair for damaged or fragile volumes. It serves as a community hub for literary events, including candlelight readings and local author signings.
