Noah Grant's Grill House & Oyster Bar is a cornerstone of Zionsville's upscale dining scene, located on the historic brick Main Street. The restaurant is widely recognized for maintaining the largest and freshest selection of oysters in central Indiana, alongside an extensive sushi menu and premium Certified Angus Beef steaks.

The interior offers a casual yet sophisticated atmosphere suitable for both special occasions and weeknight dining. In addition to its seafood specialties, the kitchen focuses on seasonal ingredients and house-made sauces. Reservations are generally recommended, particularly for weekend evening service.
