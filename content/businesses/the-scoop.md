The Scoop is a Zionsville favorite located at the southern anchor of the brick Main Street. Housed in a beautifully restored Victorian building, the shop is easily recognized by its expansive wraparound porch and outdoor patio seating. It offers a rotating selection of more than 20 gourmet ice cream flavors, including vegan and no-sugar-added options.

Its location directly across from the site of the seasonal Farmers Market makes it a popular stop for families and morning strollers. The setting provides a classic small-town experience, perfect for enjoying a cone while watching the activity in the Village.
