Gifted is a specialty boutique on Cedar Street, just off Main Street in downtown Zionsville. Described by its owners as "European-inspired," the shop carries a curated mix of accessories, children's items, home goods, candles, jewelry, gourmet products, and baskets, with items personally sourced from across Europe and the United States.

## What you'll find

The boutique stocks specialty items across several categories:

- **Handbags and accessories**
- **Children's toys**
- **Pajamas and soft goods**
- **Candles and bath products**
- **Tableware, home accessories, and stationery**
- **Jewelry**
- **Gourmet items and basket fillers**

## Gift wrapping and baskets

Gifted offers **complimentary gift wrapping** in store. The shop also sells gift baskets that customers can fill with selected items, and customers may bring their own baskets to fill.
