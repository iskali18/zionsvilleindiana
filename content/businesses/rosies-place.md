Rosie's Place is a family-owned breakfast and brunch destination located on South Main Street in the heart of the Village. Since opening in Zionsville in 2014, it has become a staple for both locals and visitors. The menu features a comprehensive range of all-day breakfast options—including custom omelets, eggs Benedict, and breakfast burritos—alongside a full lunch menu of sandwiches, salads, and house-made soups.

A signature feature of the café is the bakery case, stocked daily with their famous Gooey Butter Cookies in original, chocolate, and rotating seasonal flavors. The atmosphere is bright and welcoming, offering brunch cocktails like mimosas and Bloody Marys to complement the weekend experience.
