My Sugar Pie is a gourmet pie bakery in downtown Zionsville, located on East Pine Street just off Main. The shop bakes whole pies and pie slices in fruit, cream, classic, and seasonal flavors that rotate throughout the year.

## The pies

My Sugar Pie offers a wide range of homemade pies, including fruit pies, cream pies, custard-style pies, and seasonal specials. Pies are sold by the slice and as whole pies for take-home orders, gifts, holidays, and special occasions. The shop also publishes a printable pie menu and offers gift cards and seasonal slice specials.

## Ordering and shipping

Pies can be ordered online for pickup, and many flavors can be shipped nationwide through the shop's online store. For shipped orders, the site provides pie care and reheating instructions to keep pies at their best on arrival.

## Planning your visit

My Sugar Pie is at 40 E. Pine Street, just off Main Street in downtown Zionsville. Pickup orders ask for a chosen pickup date at checkout — check the current hours and the [order page](https://shop.mysugarpie.com/) on its website, or its [Google Maps listing](https://www.google.com/maps/place/40+E+Pine+St,+Zionsville,+IN+46077), before planning a visit.
