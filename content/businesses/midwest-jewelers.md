Midwest Jewelers and Estate Buyers is a family-owned, full-service jewelry store located at the southern entrance of Zionsville's historic Village. The 4,000-square-foot showroom features a unique interior with custom oak cases and a large saltwater aquarium, reflecting an "old-world" aesthetic that fits the community's character.

The store specializes in new and estate fine jewelry, with a particular focus on custom design and award-winning gem cutting. Their on-site goldsmiths use modern CAD technology to help clients create bespoke pieces. Beyond retail, the store offers professional appraisals, watch repair, and a transparent estate buying process for jewelry and family heirlooms.
