Angelo's Italian Market is a family-owned specialty food shop that opened its Zionsville location in late 2025. The market produces fresh, housemade pasta in various cuts and shapes, paired with signature sauces made from scratch. The inventory includes Italian cheeses, locally sourced meats, fresh-baked focaccia, and specialty dry goods such as premium olive oils and spices.

The shop provides multiple catering options, including "take and bake" meals designed for home preparation. For those interested in culinary education, private pasta-making classes are conducted in an on-site classroom. 

**Hours:** Tuesday–Friday: 10:00 am – 6:30 pm; Saturday: 10:00 am – 4:00 pm.
