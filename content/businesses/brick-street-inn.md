The Brick Street Inn is a luxury boutique hotel located on the historic brick-paved Main Street of Zionsville. The property, which dates back to 1865, offers eight fully renovated guest rooms that feature modern amenities such as fiber internet and Nespresso service. The Inn provides dedicated event spaces, including the William Zion Library, for groups of 8 to 80.

The on-site restaurant, Auberge Restaurant & Bar, serves traditional French-inspired cuisine. The menu includes classic dishes such as Beef Bourguignon and Moules Marinières, with seasonal outdoor seating available along the brick street. 

**Hours (Auberge):** Dinner served Wednesday–Saturday; Brunch served Saturday–Sunday.
