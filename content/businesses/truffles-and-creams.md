Truffles & Creams is an artisan chocolate shop located on East Pine Street, just a short walk from the main brick thoroughfare. The family-owned business specializes in small-batch production of handcrafted ganache truffles, buttery caramels, and traditional "turtles." 

The shop maintains a tradition of fine chocolate making, focusing on high-quality ingredients and seasonal creations. Visitors can customize their own gift boxes from the daily selection in the display case. For those unable to visit the Village in person, their handcrafted products are also available through their online store.
