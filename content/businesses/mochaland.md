Mochaland is a small-batch specialty roaster and bakery in the retail center near Bennett Parkway on Michigan Road. The Zionsville shop opened in mid-2026, expanding from the original West Lafayette location. The menu blends Middle Eastern coffee traditions — Turkish coffee and Arabic qahwah — with a full lineup of everyday espresso drinks, filter coffee, pour-overs, matcha, and specialty teas.

Signature lattes lean into distinctive flavor combinations, including a Mochaland Special with cardamom, honey, and house spice mix; a Pistachio latte with white chocolate; and a Lotus latte made with Biscoff cookie butter cream. Behind the counter, brass coffee pots and small finjan cups reflect the shop's Middle Eastern focus. Nearby, a "Lab Wheel" of clear vials holds coffee beans, matcha powder, and spice mixes so customers can see and smell what goes into the menu. Sweets include cheesecakes, tiramisu, crème brûlée, and a range of croissants.
