Fivethirty Home is a family-owned boutique on South Main Street specializing in a curated selection of repurposed furniture, art, home accents. The shop’s aesthetic blends contemporary rustic and industrial chic styles, focusing on unique pieces offered at accessible price points. 

As an authorized retailer of Miss Mustard Seed's Milk Paint, the shop provides the supplies and expertise needed for furniture refinishing. They also host regular milk paint classes for customers interested in learning refinishing techniques. The shop’s name honors the owner’s father and his history of collecting along the Ohio River.
