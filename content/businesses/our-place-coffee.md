Our Place Coffee is a locally owned coffee shop and café at 65 S 1st Street in downtown Zionsville, one block west of Main Street in the Village. It serves coffee, tea, breakfast, and lunch in a relaxed setting for conversation, studying, work, or meeting friends.

The café opened in October 2022 and was built as a gathering place for the community. That purpose carries through in the name — a spot where people can meet, talk, and feel comfortable coming back.

## What to expect

Our Place Coffee has a neighborhood café feel, with indoor seating, outdoor seating in warmer weather, and a location close to Main Street. It draws a mix of people in for coffee, tea, breakfast, lunch, studying, business meetups, and time with friends.

## Food and drinks

The menu covers coffee, espresso drinks, tea, and non-coffee options, along with breakfast and lunch fares. Drinks include brewed coffee, lattes, flat whites, cappuccinos, cold brew, blended drinks, smoothies, refreshers, chai, and matcha, among others.

Menu details can change, so check Our Place Coffee's current menu before making a special trip for a specific item.
