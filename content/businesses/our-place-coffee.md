Our Place Coffee is a locally owned specialty coffee shop situated one block west of Main Street on South First Street. Known for its welcoming, community-centric environment, the shop serves a variety of espresso-based drinks, pour-overs, and seasonal specialties. 

The menu also features a selection of light fare and locally sourced baked goods. The space is designed for comfort, offering a mix of seating options that cater to those looking for a quiet place to work or a casual spot for conversation. It has become a neighborhood staple for residents and visitors exploring the Village.
